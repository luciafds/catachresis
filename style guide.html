Submission Guidlines

•   Please send all submissions to: catachresisjournal@gmail.com

•    Attach your work as a .docx file (for text) or .jpg/.png (for images). If submitting multiple poems, include them in a single document, with each poem on a new page.

• Submissions should be:

         -   Typed in 12pt font

         -   1.5 or double-spaced

•    Work must be previously unpublished (including on personal blogs or social media).

 •    Simultaneous submissions are welcome, but please let us know immediately if your work is accepted elsewhere.

 •    We do not currently offer payment, but all contributors will receive a PDF copy of the issue and acknowledgment across our platforms.

Rights and Permissions

By submitting, you confirm that the work is your own, and you retain full copyright. 

As a digital and open access publisher, we will publish works under a Creative Commons 4.0 license. We will apply for the license on the behalf of the authors, and obtain express permission for licensing from all pieces selected for publishing.

Deadline

Submissions on November 1st, although this closing date may be subject to change depending on volume and quality of submissions.
